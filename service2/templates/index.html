About

Bibliomania is a Python app hosted on Google Cloud's App Engine that opens and slices text from an ebook and then posts sequentially to Twitter with a hashtag. The app uses Tweepy for Twitter authorization and Beautiful Soup for text parsing. A cron job handles the scheduling of tweets, and a blob stores the character count of the last slice's endpoint (the "bookmark").
